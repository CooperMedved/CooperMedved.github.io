What I do

I’m an MS EE student focused on digital hardware: RTL design, verification, DFT fundamentals, and FPGA prototyping. I enjoy taking a system from a clean spec → implementation → verification → measurable results.

  • RTL modules (CDC/FIFOs, crypto blocks, interfaces)

My current “flagship” direction is pairing FPGA/RTL work with DFT hooks and hardware-security experiments, then validating behavior through automation.
